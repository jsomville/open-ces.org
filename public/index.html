Welcome to Open CES

Open CES stands for Open Currency Exchange System. It is a fully open-source platform designed to facilitate seamless digital exchange of local currencies within communities.

Our mission

Our mission is to empower local economies by providing an accessible and transparent currency exchange solution. We warmly welcome developers and enthusiasts to join us in improving and expanding the platform to increase its adoption and impact.
